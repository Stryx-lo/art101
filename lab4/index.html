Psudocode:How to make Mac and Cheese

  1. Buy a box of dry Mac and Cheese from the store
  2. Read the instructions on the box
  3. Boil 6 cups of water in a pot on the stove
  4. When the water is boiling, add pasta.
  5. Boil pasta in water for 10-12 minutes.
  6. When pasta is soft, pour it into a strainer.
  7. Put pasta back in the empty pot.
  8. Add milk, butter, and cheese powder.
  9. Add extra shredded cheese for extra gooeyness.
  10. Scoop a serving into a bowl.
  11. Eat and enjoy.
